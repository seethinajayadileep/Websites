Rakesh Jhunjhunwala was an Indian billionaire business magnate, Chartered Accountant, stock trader, and investor. He began investing in 1985 with a capital of ₹5,000, with his first major profit in 1986. At the time of his death he had an estimated net worth of $5.8 billion, making him the 438th richest person in the world.

Here is a timeline of Rakesh life events:

  • Jhunjhunwala was born on 5 July 1960
  • His father Radheshyam Jhunjhunwala worked as a Commissioner of Income Tax.[
  • Jhunjhunwala's interest in stock markets arose when he observed his father discussing the markets with his friends.
  • With his savings at hand, Rakesh investing early when in college.
  • Beginning with ₹5,000 capital in 1985, Jhunjhunwala's first big profit came in the form of ₹5 lakh in 1986
  • Between 1986 and 1989, he earned almost ₹20–25 lakh profit.
  • By 2022, his investment had grown to ₹11,000 crores.[13] As of 2021, his biggest investment was in Titan Company which is worth ₹7,294.8 crore.
  • Jhunjhunwala was the chairman of Aptech and Hungama Digital Media Entertainment and sat on the board of directors of Prime Focus Limited, Geojit Financial Services, Bilcare Limited, Praj Industries, Provogue, Concord Biotech
  • In July 2021, he invested in Akasa Air, a low-cost airline in India, investing 400 million dollars for a 40% stake in the airline
  • As of January 2023, the new airline has 14 aircraft and flies to 11 cities.
  • Before his death Rakesh had increased his stake in the airline to 46% becoming the largest stakeholder in the company.
